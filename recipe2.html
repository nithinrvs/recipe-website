Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/4 cups (280g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup (100g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (200g) brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 cups (350g) chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (180°C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda. Set it aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until the mixture is light and fluffy.
  4. Beat in the eggs, one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop rounded tablespoons of cookie dough onto the prepared baking sheet. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the edges are golden but the centers are still soft.
  7. Let the cookies cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
