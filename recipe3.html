Vegetable Stir-Fry Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups (240g) mixed vegetables (e.g., bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, snow peas)
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, grated
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 cups (400g) cooked rice or noodles

Instructions:

  1. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Add minced garlic and grated ginger to the hot oil. Saut√© for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
  3. Add the mixed vegetables to the skillet and stir-fry for 3-5 minutes until they are tender-crisp.
  4. Pour in the soy sauce and sesame oil. Stir well to coat the vegetables evenly.
  5. Continue to stir-fry for an additional 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  6. Serve the vegetable stir-fry over cooked rice or noodles. Enjoy!
